Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Lua: Cycle Tracker ("Lua", "we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Lua does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you enter into the app, including cycle logs, journal entries, and settings, is stored locally on your device only.

No Accounts

Lua does not require you to create an account. There is no sign-up, login, or authentication process.

No Analytics or Tracking

Lua does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or location information.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we do not share, sell, or transfer any information to third parties.

Data Storage

All your data remains on your device. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Lua does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
